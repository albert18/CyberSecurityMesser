******** nestat
-Network statistics
-netstat -a
    -Show all active connections
-netstat -b
    -Show binaries
-netstat -n
    -Do not resolve names

******** traceroutes
-Determine the route a packet takes to a destination
-Takes advantage of ICMP time to live exceede error message
-Not all devices will reply ICMP time exceeded messages

********nslookup and dig
-Lookup information from DNS servers
-nslookup
-dig

******** Address Resolution Protocol
-Determiing a MAC address
-arp -a
    -View local ARP table
