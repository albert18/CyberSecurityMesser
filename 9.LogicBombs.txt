----------------------------------- Logic Bomb
*Waits for a predefined event
-Often left by someone with grudge

*Time Bomb
-Time or Date

*User event
-Logic bomb

*Difficult to recover if it goes off

----------------------------------- Preventing a logic bomb
*Difficult to recognize
-Each is unique
-No predefined signatures

*Process and procedures
-Format change control

*Electronic monitoring
-Alert on changes
-Host-baed intrusion detection, tripwire, etc.

*Constant auditing
-An administrator can circumvent existing systems
